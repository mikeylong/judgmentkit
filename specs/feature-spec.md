# Feature Spec Template

## Activity

Name the activity being supported before naming the feature or screen.

## Participants

Name the people, agents, reviewers, or systems participating in the activity.

## Objective

State the objective shared by the participants.

## Outcomes

List the concrete outcomes the activity should produce.

## Existing Tools And Artifacts

List tools, documents, workflows, prototypes, examples, systems, or handoff materials already involved.

## Rules And Rituals

Describe review rituals, decision rules, handoffs, approvals, or constraints that already shape the activity.

## Division Of Labor

Name who decides, who contributes, who reviews, who implements, and who receives the result.
